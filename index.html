Sree Poornathrayeesha Balasramam is a charitable institution dedicated to providing shelter, care, and support to destitute boys. Affiliated with Swami Vivekanandha Cultural Society, we are a voluntary social welfare organization under the umbrella of Viswa Hindu Parishad.
